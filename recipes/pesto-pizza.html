Pesto Pizza

Description

Pesto pizza is a simple and great alternative to your regular pizza.

Ingredients

  1. 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  2. ½ cup pesto
  3. 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  4. ½ cup green bell pepper, chopped
  5. 1 (2 ounce) can chopped black olives, drained
  6. ½ small red onion, chopped
  7. 1 (4 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and sliced
  8. 1 cup crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  • Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  • Spread pesto on pizza crust. Top with tomato, bell pepper, olives, red onion, artichoke hearts, and feta cheese.
  • Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and browned, 8 to 10 minutes.

Tip

Serve with vegetables
